tandoori chicken

Tandoori chicken is a flavorful dish originating from the Indian subcontinent, characterized by marinated chicken cooked in a tandoor, a clay oven. The marinade typically includes yogurt, lemon juice, and a blend of spices like cumin, coriander, and garam masala, resulting in tender, succulent chicken with a vibrant red hue and smoky flavor. It's a beloved dish known for its aromatic spices and juicy, charred exterior.
